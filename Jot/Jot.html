This is an editable example showing how to use the Jot editor.

Bold (supports ctrl-b/cmd-b keyboard shortcut)

Italics (supports ctrl-i/cmd-i keyboard shortcut)

Blockquotes are supported.

Jots can be saved using ctrl-s/cmd-s keyboard shortcut or using the ↓ button. Saving a jot stores it in your browser's local storage. Depending on your browser settings, the saved data may potentially be cleared when you close your browser.
